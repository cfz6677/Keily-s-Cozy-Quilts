I was born in Arizona but have been relocated several times throughout my life. My husband and I moved to Arizona in July of 2014 to be closer to family and to start a family of our own. We love spending time outdoors at soccer games, hiking and swimming. I can remember watching my mom make our clothes and blankets even as a young kid. I made my first quilt all by myself for my husband’s Yia-Yia. I enjoyed it way more than I thought I would and of course Yia-Yia loved it! 2014 is where my quilting journey truly began. My mom and I took the plunge and purchased a Gammill Statler Stitcher in 2018. We fell in love immediately! We are the perfect team. My mom is an amazing piecer and I do all the Gammill Statler Quilting. We love working together and passing on the tradition. My girls and nieces even show an interest! I would be honored to complete your beautiful piecing work with my quilting.
